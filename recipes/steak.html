Steak with Potatoes and Veggies

Description

Steak with mashed potatoes and veggies is a staple meal that you can customize to your personal
preference. The steak mostly stays the same, but you can swap the veggies for whatever
your favorite veggies are, or just what you have in the fridge. My go-to are brussle
sprouts and asparagus, but I've used corn, carrots, mushrooms often find their way in, as well
as broccoli, green beans, peas, whatever you got!. As for the potatoes, you can make mashed
potatoes, baked potatoes, fries, whatever you want. For this recipe, we'll stick to my favorites,
which are mashed potatoes with asparagus and brussle sprouts. This recipe does require an oven
safe pan for the steak, but you can just cook the steak fully on the stove if needed.

Ingredients

  • Steak of your choice, I usually do NY strip
  • 4-5 russet potatoes
  • 1/2 a bundle of asparagus
  • 1 cup of brussle sprouts
  • 1/4 cup of whole milk
  • 1/3 stick of butter
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Directions

  1. To start, this recipe has a little prep work
    • Preheat your oven to 425
    • Pat the steak dry and season both sides with salt & pepper. Let it chill on the counter
      while you prep the veggies.
    • Peel, rinse, and dice your potatoes
    • Cut the bottoms of the brussle sprouts, remove any yucky leafs, and cut them in half
    • Remove the bottoms of the asparagus. If you bend them from the bottom, they will just snap off.
  2. For the potatoes:
    1. Add them to a pot, cover with water, and boil
    2. Once they're tender all the way through, strain them and return them to the
      pot, but with the heat off
    3. Add the butter in small chunks along with the milk and mash
    4. Season with salt & pepper and you're done.
  3. For the veggies:
    1. Add your veggies to a bowl with the oil and salt & pepper
    2. Toss them in the oil and spread them on a baking sheet
    3. Cover the baking sheet tightly with foil and bake for 20-25 minutes
    4. You'll bake them for an additional 10 minutes with the steak
  4. For the steak:
    1. To a hot pan, add some oil and sear the steak on one side for about 4 minutes
    2. Flip the steak, cover it and put the pan in the oven with the veggies for 10 minutes.
    3. Remove the steak, put it on a plate, and let it rest for about 10 minutes
  5. Finally, add everything to the plate with the steak. I like to pour the juices on the
    potatoes and veggies.
